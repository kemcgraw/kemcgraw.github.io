Biography

Welcome to my academic portfolio. I am a postdoctoral research scientist in the Department of Environmental Health Sciences at Columbia University Mailman School of Public Health, where my research focuses on the cardiovascular impacts from under regulated pollutants and toxic chemicals. I am passionate about advancing actionable science, applying and interpreting advanced statistical methods, and mentoring the next generation of researchers.

My work spans multiple areas including cardiovascular disease, tobacco and cannabis use, environmental exposures, and public health. I am committed to making research more transparent, accessible, and reproducible through the development of open-source tools and educational resources.
